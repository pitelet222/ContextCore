# Customer Support Policy

This document describes the support tiers, response-time SLAs, and
escalation process for all Northwind Robotics customers. Support tiers
correspond to the product tiers described in the product lineup.

## Support tiers and response times

| Tier        | Applies to | Critical issue response | General ticket response |
|-------------|-----------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| Standard    | Scout     | 8 business hours         | 2 business days |
| Priority    | Hauler    | 4 business hours         | 1 business day |
| Enterprise  | Forge     | 1 hour, 24/7              | 4 business hours |

"Critical issue" means a robot fleet is fully halted (zero functioning units)
or there is a safety incident.

## How support is staffed

General tickets are handled by the Krakow support office during European
business hours, and by a smaller on-call rotation outside those hours for
Priority and Enterprise customers. Critical issues for Enterprise customers
are routed directly to an on-call engineer regardless of time zone.

## Escalation process

If a customer is unsatisfied with the resolution of a ticket, or if a Forge
(Enterprise) customer needs to request an early contract termination or
exception to the standard refund policy described in the product lineup, the
request must be escalated to the VP of Customer Success, Elena Ruiz, who has
authority to approve exceptions up to $50,000 in contract value. Larger
exceptions require sign-off from the CEO, Priya Anand.

## Refund exceptions

Refunds outside the standard policy (e.g., waiving the early-termination fee
on a Forge contract) are evaluated case-by-case during the escalation
process above. Common approved reasons include extended hardware downtime
(more than 5 consecutive business days) caused by a manufacturing defect.

## Software issues

Bugs or outages in NorthwindOS (any tier — Basic, Pro, or Enterprise) are
treated as general tickets unless they cause a "Critical issue" as defined
above (i.e., they halt an entire robot fleet).
