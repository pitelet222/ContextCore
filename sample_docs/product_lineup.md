# Product Lineup and Pricing

Northwind Robotics offers three product tiers, all of which run on the
NorthwindOS software platform described in the company overview.

## Scout (Entry tier)

The Scout unit is a compact inventory-scanning robot designed for warehouses
under 50,000 square feet. It does not perform picking or packing, only
shelf-scanning and stock-level reporting.

- Price: $1,200/month per unit, minimum 2-unit fleet
- Includes: NorthwindOS Basic dashboard, weekly inventory reports
- Support tier: Standard (see support policy)

## Hauler (Mid tier)

The Hauler unit adds autonomous transport between picking stations and
packing stations. It is the most popular product and accounts for roughly 60%
of deployed units.

- Price: $2,800/month per unit, minimum 4-unit fleet
- Includes: NorthwindOS Pro dashboard, real-time fleet tracking, monthly
  optimization reports
- Support tier: Priority (see support policy)

## Forge (Enterprise tier)

The Forge unit is a full picking, packing, and transport robot intended for
large distribution centers (150,000+ sq ft). Forge deployments are always
sold as part of an Enterprise contract that bundles hardware, software, and a
dedicated customer success manager.

- Price: Custom, typically $4,500–$6,000/month per unit depending on fleet
  size and contract length
- Includes: NorthwindOS Enterprise (custom integrations, SSO, audit logs),
  dedicated onboarding team, quarterly business reviews
- Support tier: Enterprise (see support policy) — includes a 1-hour critical
  response SLA

## Refunds and contract changes

All tiers are billed monthly in arrears. Customers on the Scout and Hauler
tiers may downgrade or cancel with 30 days' written notice. Forge
(Enterprise) contracts are annual commitments; early termination requires
approval per the escalation process described in the support policy, and may
incur an early-termination fee of up to 20% of the remaining contract value.
